    Good to know

    Questions, answered.

    Do I need my own camera to join a physical class?

    No. Studio cameras and lighting are available during class. If you own gear, bring it — we'll teach you to get the most out of what you have.

    Where do physical classes hold?

    At the TooAmple studio in Lagos. Cohort dates and the full schedule are shared once your application is confirmed.

    How do I receive digital products after payment?

    Once your payment is confirmed (card or bank transfer — both verify automatically), your download is delivered to the email you provide at checkout, usually within minutes.

    Can I pay by bank transfer?

    Yes. Our checkout supports card, USSD and bank transfer — transfers are verified automatically, so you don't need to send proof of payment.

    Do you offer payment plans for tuition?

    Yes, for physical classes. Mention it in your application and we'll set up a plan with you.
