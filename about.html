Who we are

Built to make you unforgettable.

TooAmple Studios is a premium photography and cinematography studio based in Lagos, Nigeria. We exist for one reason — to turn real moments into work that stops people mid-scroll.

Founded by Temidayo Omoniyi Fredrick, TooAmple was born out of a belief that Lagos deserves world-class visual storytelling. From intimate studio portraits to full-scale wedding films, every project is approached with a director's eye, a cinematographer's precision and a photographer's heart.

We work with individuals, couples, brands and artists who understand that great visuals aren't a luxury — they're leverage.

The founder

Temidayo Omoniyi Fredrick

Temidayo is a creative director, photographer, videographer and AI filmmaker whose work spans studio portraiture, cinematic wedding films, brand commercials and Nigeria's emerging AI film space. Based in Lagos, he founded TooAmple Studios as a space where visual quality meets intentional storytelling.

His commercial work includes campaigns for itel Mobile, digifon and Fair & White Paris — bringing the same cinematic discipline to brand content that he applies to weddings and personal shoots. Under his REGISSEUR AI imprint, he produces AI-generated Nigerian blockbusters that are redefining how African stories are told on screen.

Alongside the studio, Temidayo founded the TooAmple Academy — hands-on, small-cohort training in photography, cinematography and editing, designed to build the next generation of Lagos visual storytellers.
